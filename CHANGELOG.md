## Getting Started

1. **Clone the repository**
2. **Open in Xcode**: `open WebsiteToiOSApp.xcodeproj`
3. **Build and run**: Press Cmd+R
4. **Configure AI services** in Settings
5. **Create your first project** and start converting!
